The Year of the Ox is the second in the Chinese Zodiac cycle.

Ox years: 1913, 1925, 1937, 1949, 1961, 1973, 1985, 1997, 2009, 2021...

Oxes are known for diligence, dependability, strength and determination.

Best with: Rat, Monkey, or Rooster

Worst with: Tiger, Dragon, Horse, or Goat
