The Year of the Rooster is tenth in the Chinese Zodiac cycle.

Rooster years: 1921, 1933, 1945, 1957, 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, 2017, and 2029...

Roosters are hardworking, resourceful, courageous and talented.

Best with: Ox or Snake

Worst with: Rat, Rabbit, Horse, or Pig
